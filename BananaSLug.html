Banana Slug!

Nature's Slimy Yellow Friend


Banana Slug Wiki

The banana slug, scientific name Ariolimax columbianus is native to North America's Pacific coastal coniferous rainforest belt. While it may not seem like it, this includes California! Banana slugs are detritivores, feasting on plants, animal droppings, dead plant material and more, before returning them to the soil. Their slime is known to cause numbness on predators' tongues.
